FAQ

How can I play chess with my friends?

Just create a new private session and copy&paste and share URL (looks like; multiplayerchess.com/#!/f0oB4r) of the session.

Can I bookmark the games/sessions?

Yes! Please don't hestitate to archive them.

Can I use my Facebook and/or Twitter Id?

Next version will cover public profiles and related options.

Will it always be free?

Yes. There'll be no paid-accounts, no ads, no evil trackers.

Is it possible to export a game?

Yes, you can get the PGN output (see game buttons in sidebar)

What're the features you're planning to implement for the next version?

Profiles takes the first line. Draw agreement, PGN import, replay and e-mail service are the other major features I'll implement.

What are the ways of contributing this website?

You may buy me a drink, help me spread multiplayerchess.com to other people, share your thoughts and advices... If you're a coder willing to contribute the project, please don't hestitate to request the source code from me. I'm already checking out the ways of open sourcing it.
